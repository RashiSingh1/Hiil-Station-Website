Gulmarg

known as the "Meadow of Flowers," is a premier hill station and top-tier skiing destination in Jammu and Kashmir's Baramulla district, located 52 km from Srinagar. Situated at 2,690 meters.

Top Attraction in Gulmarg

Khilanmarg

Khilanmarg is a stunning, high-altitude meadow in Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir, situated 4-6 km from Gulmarg town. Famous for panoramic Himalayan views (Nanga Parbat, Nun Kun).

Alpather Lake

Alpather Lake, known as the "Frozen Lake," is a stunning high-altitude alpine lake located 13 km from Gulmarg, Kashmir, at the foot of the twin Apharwat Peaks (approx. 4,380 meters)

Apharwat Peak

Located in Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir, is a premier tourist destination rising to 4,390 metres ( ft). Known for hosting Asia’s longest ski slopes and year-round snow, it is accessed via the second phase of the Gulmarg Gondola.

Gulmarg Gondola

The Gulmarg Gondola is one of the world's highest and longest cable car rides, located in Jammu and Kashmir, India. It is a two-phase, high-altitude ropeway that carries visitors from the Gulmarg resort up to the Apharwat mountain peak.

Maharani Temple

Historic Maharani Temple (or Rani Temple) in Gulmarg, Jammu and Kashmir, is a 1915-built shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, constructed by Maharani Mohini Bai Sisodia, wife of Dogra King Hari Singh. Situated on a small hillock.

Best Time To Visit

Spring (Mar–May)

  • Temperature: 10–20°C
  • Snow slowly melts and flowers bloom
  • Beautiful green meadows appear
  • Perfect for sightseeing and nature walks

Summer (Jun–Aug)

  • Temperature: 15–25°C
  • Pleasant and cool weather
  • Best for trekking and outdoor activities
  • Clear views of mountains

Autumn (Sep–Nov)

  • Temperature: 10–18°C
  • Crisp air and fewer tourists
  • Scenic landscapes and peaceful atmosphere
  • Great time for photography

Winter (Dec–Feb)

  • Temperature: -4 to 10°C
  • Heavy snowfall and snow-covered landscapes
  • Best time for skiing and snowboarding
  • Perfect for winter sports lovers
