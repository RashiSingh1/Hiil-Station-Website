Pahalgam

A stunning, serene village in Jammu and Kashmir's Ganderbal district, located at 2,758 meters, known for breathtaking views of the Kinner Kailash range, apple orchards, and rich local culture.

Top Attraction in Pahalgam

Betaab Valley

Betaab Valley, located 15 km from Pahalgam in Jammu and Kashmir’s Anantnag district, is a breathtaking scenic spot named after the 1983 Bollywood film Betaab.

Chandanwari

Chandanwari, located about 16 km from Pahalgam in Jammu & Kashmir at 2,895 meters, is a scenic valley known as the starting point of the annual Amarnath Yatra. Famed for its year-round snow bridge, Lidder River views.

Tulian Lake

A stunning, high-altitude alpine lake situated at approximately 3,353 to 3,684 meters (12,087 ft) near Pahalgam in Kashmir, nestled between the Zanskar and Pir Panjal ranges

Mamleshwar Temple

The Mamleshwar Temple, located on the banks of the Lidder River in Pahalgam, Kashmir, is an ancient 12th-century shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. Known for its unique Kashmiri stone architecture with a two-tiered roof, the temple is historically significant.

Aru Valley

Aru Valley is a picturesque valley located near Pahalgam in Jammu and Kashmir, known for its lush greenery, scenic beauty, and tranquil atmosphere.

Best Time To Visit

Spring (Mar–May)

  • Temperature: 8–20°C
  • Snow melts and flowers bloom
  • Beautiful green meadows appear
  • Perfect for sightseeing and nature walks

Summer (Jun–Aug)

  • Temperature: 15–28°C
  • Pleasant and cool weather
  • Best time for trekking and camping
  • Clear views of surrounding mountains

Autumn (Sep–Nov)

  • Temperature: 5–18°C
  • Golden forests and crisp air
  • Fewer tourists and peaceful atmosphere
  • Great time for photography and hiking

Winter (Dec–Feb)

  • Temperature: -8 to 5°C
  • Heavy snowfall covers the valley
  • Stunning snow-covered pine forests
  • Perfect for snow lovers and couples

Travel Tips

🧥

Carry Warm Clothes

Pahalgam weather can turn cold even in summers due to high altitude. Always carry warm jackets, woolen layers, and a light raincoat for unexpected weather changes.

👟

Wear Comfortable Trekking Shoes

Pahalgam has famous treks like Baisaran and Aru Valley. Wear sturdy trekking shoes with good grip to walk safely on the grassy and rocky mountain trails.

🐴

Try a Horse Ride

Pahalgam is famous for its scenic horse riding to Baisaran meadows. Do not miss this local activity to explore the beautiful valleys and lush green pastures.

📷

Carry a Camera

Pahalgam offers stunning views of the Lidder River, pine forests, and snow-capped peaks. Bring a camera to capture the breathtaking landscapes and unforgettable Kashmiri scenery.
