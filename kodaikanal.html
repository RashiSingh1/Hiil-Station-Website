Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal, known as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a serene, verdant destination in Tamil Nadu's Western Ghats, located ~7,000 ft above sea level

Top Attraction in kodaikanal

Kurinji Temple

Kurinji Temple, located in Kodaikanal, is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, the Hindu god of war. The temple is named after the Kurinji flower, which blooms once every 12 years in the surrounding hills.

Mannavanur

Mannavanur, located about 34 km from Kodaikanal in Tamil Nadu, is a tranquil hamlet known for its stunning, serene lake, surrounding pine forests, and rolling grasslands.

Coakers Walk

Coaker's Walk in Kodaikanal is a 1-km scenic pedestrian path built in 1872 along steep southern mountain slopes, offering breathtaking, panoramic views of valleys and plains from 2,000 ft

Silver Cascade Falls

Silver Cascade Falls, located approximately 8 kilometers from Kodaikanal, is a beautiful waterfall that offers a refreshing retreat amidst lush green forests and misty hills. The cascading waters of the falls create a picturesque setting, making it a popular tourist attraction for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Pillar Rocks

Pillar Rocks, situated in the picturesque hill station of Kodaikanal, are a set of three massive granite rock pillars that stand majestically at an elevation of approximately 400 feet. These natural wonders are a popular tourist attraction, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys, forests, and mist-covered hills.

Best Time To Visit

Autumn (Sep–Nov)

  • Temperature: 15–25°C
  • Clear skies and pleasant weather
  • Best time for trekking and hiking
  • Perfect for nature photography

Spring (Mar–May)

  • Temperature: 15–25°C
  • Pleasant weather with blooming flowers
  • Best time for sightseeing and lake visits
  • Perfect for nature walks

Summer (Jun–Aug)

  • Temperature: 20–30°C
  • Cool escape from summer heat
  • Ideal for boating and exploring viewpoints
  • Perfect for water activities

Winter (Dec–Feb)

  • Temperature: 8 to 18°C
  • Cold and misty mornings
  • Peaceful atmosphere with fewer tourists
  • Perfect for relaxing hill vacations

Travel Tips

🧥

Carry Light Warm Clothes

Weather in Kodaikanal can be cool, especially in mornings and evenings. Carry light jackets or sweaters.

👟

Wear ComfortableShoes

Kodaikanal has many walking trails and viewpoints. Comfortable shoes will help you explore easily.

🌧

Carry Rain Protection

Rain showers are common in the hills. Carry an umbrella or raincoat while exploring.

📷

Carry a Camera

Kodaikanal is known for scenic lakes, forests, and viewpoints. Don’t forget your camera to capture the beauty of nature.
