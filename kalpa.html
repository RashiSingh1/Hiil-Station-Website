Kalpa

A stunning, serene village in Himachal Pradesh's Kinnaur district, located at 2,758 meters, known for breathtaking views of the Kinner Kailash range, apple orchards, and rich local culture.

Top Attraction in Kalpa

Kinnaur Kailash

Kinnaur Kailash (or Kinner Kailash) is a sacred 6,050-meter high mountain in Himachal Pradesh, India, revered by Hindus and Buddhists as a mythical abode of Lord Shiva and Parvati.

Narayan-Nagini Temple

An ancient, revered shrine located in Chini village, dedicated to Lord Vishnu (Narayan) and the serpent goddess Nagini

Kamru Fort

Located near Sangla in the Kinnaur district (often associated with nearby Kalpa), is a stunning 15th-century tower-like structure located on a hilltop

Baspa Valley

The Baspa Valley, also known as the Sangla or Tukpa Valley, is a scenic river valley in Himachal Pradesh's Kinnaur District, renowned for the pristine Baspa River, snow-capped peaks, and lush forests.

Sapni Fort

Sapni Fort is a historic seven-story, timber-laced tower located near Kalpa in Himachal Pradesh’s Kinnaur district.

Best Time To Visit

Spring (Mar–May)

  • Temperature: 5–18°C
  • Apple orchards in full bloom
  • Beautiful snow-capped Kinner Kailash views
  • Perfect for nature walks and photography

Summer (Jun–Aug)

  • Temperature: 15–25°C
  • Pleasant and cool weather
  • Best for trekking and outdoor activities
  • Clear views of mountains

Autumn (Sep–Nov)

  • Temperature: 5–15°C
  • Apple harvest season begins
  • Golden landscapes and crisp air
  • Great time for sightseeing and photography

Winter (Dec–Feb)

  • Temperature: -10 to 5°C
  • Heavy snowfall covers the village
  • Surreal snow-covered Kinner Kailash views
  • Peaceful and offbeat winter experience

Travel Tips

🧥

Carry Heavy Winter Clothes

Weather in Kalpa can be extremely cold, especially from October to March. Always carry warm jackets, woolen layers, gloves, and thermal wear to stay comfortable.

👟

Wear Sturdy Trekking Shoes

Kalpa has rocky mountain trails and uneven paths. Wear good grip trekking shoes or hiking boots to walk safely on the hilly terrain and narrow village roads.

🍎

Try Fresh Local Apples

Kalpa is famous for its apple orchards in Kinnaur. Do not miss the chance to taste fresh apples, dried fruits, and local Kinnauri products directly from the orchards.

📷

Carry a Camera

Kalpa offers breathtaking views of Kinner Kailash peak and snow-covered landscapes. Bring a camera to capture the stunning sunrises, orchards, and Himalayan scenery.
